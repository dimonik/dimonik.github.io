Features

Information

The application presents detailed information about each work of art displayed at the exhibition: images, biography of the artist, summary of his/her career etc.

Audio Guide

In order not to divert user's gaze from the pieces of art, we added AUDIO GUIDE feature. Now it's possible to wander in the art hall contemplating the beauty and LISTENing to the extras about the works and their creators.

Layout & Directions

It's easy to plan your visit and effectively navigate through the exhibition halls checking the interactive layout. Easily locate the piece of art, artist or yourself with the help of simple search.
