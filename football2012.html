Features

Matches, Statistics & Live Streams

The application was designed to provide quick and easy access to the major events of the championship: calendar of matches, fixture list, fresh charts of the top-scored shooters and most rude bullies. And above all, it supported real-time text streams of football matches.

News & Headlines

In partnership with Korrespondent.net the app placed fresh news of the most thrilling football events of EURO 2012. In addition, handy announcements of the upcoming matches right on the starting screen insured that users would never miss the long-expected game.

History & Facts

"Football 2012" was designed to keep users engaged even when nothing was going on on the field. The app included built-in information about the history and reality of the championship: dates, heroes, teams, stadiums, medals and more.
